Lessons

Level 2-3

Sven and Sylvin teach this new level for people who have been attending a year of regular classes.
They go to numerous training courses and workshops in Belgium (Liège), France (Paris, Lille, Reims, Lyon, La Grande Motte,...), the UK (London, Birmingham), Sweden (Stockholm) and elsewhere with internationally renowned professionals, in order to constantly improve and make you progress.

Classes are scheduled on Mondays and start at 8.15pm with a small practice, just after the Level 1 – Beginners class
The first class is on on September 14th 2015.

Lessons take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran, rue de la digue 10 in 1050 Ixelles.

You do not have to register with a partner. We will take care of having en even number of leaders and followers.
