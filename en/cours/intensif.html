West Coast Swing crash course for beginners

Brussels West Coast Swing proposes from time to time a West Coast Swing crash course over a weekend, aiming dancers that are familiar with couple dances (Salsa, Tango, Ballroom, etc).

These crash courses are always followed by an afternoon of social dancing and practice in a friendly atmosphere.

At the moment, no new session is scheduled. You can find below a typical schedule for these crash courses. If you happen to be interested, please do not hesitate to contact us and share your interest, and we will make sure to contact you as soon as a new session is organized.

Schedule:
  • Saturday
    • 1.30pm to 3.30pm : Courses
    • 3.30pm to 4.30pm : Dancing break with snack offered
    • 4.30pm to 6.30pm : Courses
  • Sunday
    • 10.30am to 12.30pm : Courses
    • 12.30pm to 2pm : Break
    • 2pm to 6.30pm : Afternoon of social dancing and practice

Do not hesitate to contact us, should you have any questions.
