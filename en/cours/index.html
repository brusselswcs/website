Introduction

West Coast Swing originated and grew in the 20th century, but has really boomed since 2000. It is a relatively new dance that follows the musical trends and as such allows you to dance on contemporary music, basically everything you hear on the radio.

West Coast Swing is being danced all around the world. The country with the biggest, most dominant scene is the United States, with big West Coast Swing centers like California and Texas. In Europe, West Coast Swing is most popular in France, Russia, the UK, Hungary, etc. Still quite unknown in Belgium, this dance deserves to be known and allows for both partners to improvise steps while dancing together, putting West Coast Swing in a short list of dances that put a premium on improvisation.

We do not claim being dance teachers. We just want to genuinely share our passion for this dance and help to develop a West Coast Swing community in Brussels and in Belgium through different means detailed on this web site.
