Weekly classes prices

  • 8 lessons
    • 70€
    • 65€ for Ixelles’ residents, students or job-seekers.
  • 32 lessons
    • 215€
    • 210€ for Ixelles’ residents, students or job-seekers.

The prices include the subscription of 10 euros to Brussels WCS asbl as well as access to social dancing of all our Sunday Workshops and practice and our practice nights (Swingplicity Brussels event is not included).

Students who attend a class can also attend another class of lower level if they want to, without extra cost, provided there are places left. However, the focus remains on the students of the aforementioned level.

A sponsor system is put in place this year. You can sponsor friends (as long as they register together) and receive a discount on your own registration fees :

  • If you register for a whole year :
    • 20 euros discount per sponsored couple who registers for a whole year ;
    • 5 euros discount per sponsored couple who registers for the first 8 classes.
  • If you register for 8 classes :
    • 5 euros discount per sponsored couple who registers.

To ensure everything goes smoothly, here are the rules that we apply for these discounts :

  • discounts only applies when the payment of the sponsored couple has been received ;
  • discount only applies as long as there are places available in the class ;
  • discount applies until your registration is free ;
  • the people registering as a couple have to mention your name as a sponsor during registration ;
  • We do not refund registration fees once the year has started;
  • the sponsored couple registers for the first time to a Brussels WCS class.
