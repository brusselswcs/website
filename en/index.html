Brussels West Coast Swing asbl

Come and discover West Coast Swing, a partner dance with roots in Lindy Hop coming straight from the US. This dance allows for both partners to improvise steps while dancing together and may be danced to almost any music, pop, funk, dance, country, jazz, blues, RnB, disco...
