Who are we?

We are four Brussels residents who share the passion of West Coast Swing, a relatively new dance in Europe with roots in Lindy Hop, though now visually very different, and danced to almost any music, from old blues to modern pop.

This dance is still not or little known in Brussels. That is why we created Brussels WCS to promote this dance in Brussels and its surroundings, and also to strengthen the West Coast Swing community by forging links with other dance associations and clubs.

Since January 2013, we have been organizing Workshops and practice. Given the success, we have decided to broaden our activities. Since September 2013, we propose a beginners class and crash course sessions over a weekend.
This year 2014-2015, we create a level 2 class to continuously meet your demands.
