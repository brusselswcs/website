Workshops and practice

Concept

Several times during the year, Brussels West Coast Swing gives you the opportunity to take workshops, usually for people who have been attending a whole year of regular classes, followed by an afternoon of social dancing.
They are taught by different teachers coming from other dancing schools, who are sharing the same passion. On special occasions, other levels may be proposed.
Participation fees to our Sundays afternoon take into account drinks and snacks.

The afternoon of social dancing is open to all the people who want to practice WCS and share their passion on the dance floor.

You have to be a member of Brussels WCS asbl to take part in Brussels WCS Workshops and practice.
Subscription is 10€ and covers music royalties (SABAM, rémunération équitable) and insurance. You can become affiliated to Brussels WCS asbl during your first participation at one of our workshops and practice.

Social dancing practice will be organized whenever we do not organize special Sunday with workshops and social dancing.
