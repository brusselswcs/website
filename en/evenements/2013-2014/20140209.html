Workshops and practice

February 9th 2014 – Attitude West Coast Swing

Attitude West Coast Swing is an association which proposes West Coast Swing lessons near Lille (France). Since January 2013, AWCS do us the honour of teaching some of our workshops during our monthly Workshops and practice.

We will have the pleasure of welcoming Mikl and Pauline or Valérie for this workshop.

  • 1pm to 2pm: intermediate workshop (more than one year of lessons)

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
