Workshops and practice

January 19th 2014 – 1 year Brussels WCS

Sonya Dessureault, Quebecois teacher of West Coast Swing, do us the honour of coming to Brussels in January 2014.
More information about Sonya: StephSonya Dance
Here is a little teaser of Sonya:

We have the pleasure of having her teach us three workshops:
  • 10am to 11pm: inter+ workshop (more than two years of lessons)
  • 11am to 12pm: theme workshop "Body Isolation" (more than one years of lessons)
  • 1pm to 2pm: intermediate workshop (more than one year of lessons)
  • 1pm to 2pm: novice workshop with Amandine and Sven

Workshops will be given in French, translation in English is possible if needed.
The afternoon of social dancing will begin just after the end of the intermediate workshop and will finish around 6.30pm.

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
