Workshops and practice

September 1st 2013

Amandine, Isaline, Sven and Sylvin invite you to the Maison de Quartier Malibran at 1 pm for a free introduction to West Coast Swing.

This introduction is followed by an afternoon of training and social dancing from 2.30pm to 6.30pm. You will then be able to see people who have been dancing WCS for several years share their passion on the dancefloor.

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
