Workshops and practice

May 11th 2014 – Judith Werkman & Sven Allegaert

Judith had been dancing ballet and salsa, when she suddenly met with west coast swing in 2013. She immediately loved the dance for its creativity, teamplay and technique. Starting with her first event in Lyon, she travelled and attended Workshops and practice and classes wtih the best teachers around the US and Europe. She started giving lessons together with Maarten in Utrecht, and is eager to share with you what she has learned with the growing community in Holland.

Sven started dancing West Coast Swing in 2012 and immediately became addicted to this dance offering so many possibilities. He often goes to international Workshops and practice everywhere in Europe.
Since September 2013, he has also been giving a beginner’s class with Amandine in Brussels. He also started competing in 2013 and became Intermediate in November of last year. He recently won first place in J’n’J Intermediate at West in Lyon 2014.

They have the pleasure of teaching you one workshop:

  • 1pm to 2pm: workshop "Go from 6 counts to 8 or from 8 to 6" - Novice/Inter (more than six months of lessons)

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
