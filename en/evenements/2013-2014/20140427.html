Workshops and practice

April 27th 2014 – Céline Sully and Sven Allegaert

Céline lives in Lyon (one of the main places of West Coast Swing in Europe) and discovered this dance a few years ago. She dances in the Advanced category now and is a few points short of becoming an All-Star, she rocks! She often places in the top 5 of Jack’n’Jill and Strictly competitions in big European and American Workshops and practice, and she recently placed 1st in J’n’J Advanced/All Star at West In Lyon 2014!
She has also won in 2010 the prestigious Bob Preiss Social Dance Award, which rewards the most social dancer (the one who invites and dances with everyone) at Swing Diego, one of the biggest Workshops and practice in the USA! Her kindness and gracefulness makes her an amazing dancer and we are very happy to welcome her in Brussels.

Sven started dancing West Coast Swing in 2012 and immediately became addicted to this dance offering so many possibilities. He often goes to international Workshops and practice everywhere in Europe.
Since September 2013, he has also been giving a beginner’s class with Amandine in Brussels. He also started competing in 2013 and became Intermediate in November of last year. He recently won first place in J’n’J Intermediate at West in Lyon 2014.

We have the pleasure of having them teach us two workshops:
  • 11am to 12pm: intermediate workshop (more than one years of lessons)
  • 1pm to 2pm: beginners/intermediate workshop (more than 6 month of lessons)

Workshops will be given in French, translation in English is possible if needed.
The afternoon of social dancing will begin just after the end of the workshops and will finish around 6.30pm.

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
