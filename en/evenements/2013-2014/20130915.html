September 15th 2013 – Doug & Nicki Silton

Doug & Nicki Silton, American professionals of West Coast Swing, do us the honour of coming to Brussels in September 2013.
More information: The Siltons
Here is a little teaser of Doug & Nicki Silton:

    We have the pleasure of having them teach us two workshops:
  • 11am to 12pm: advanced workshop (more than two years of lessons)
  • 1pm to 2pm: intermediate workshop (more than one year of lessons)

Workshops will be given in English, translation in French is possible if needed.
The afternoon of social dancing will begin just after the end of the intermediate workshop and will finish around 6.30pm.

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
