Workshops and practice

March 8th 2015 – Lena and Olivier

Lena is joint manager of the “Temple du Swing” school in Paris and choreographer/responsible for the swing dance company “Les Rats de Cave”. While making dance her job, she has been dancing on a stage for 30 years and teaching for 20 years.
Olivier has a salsa and be-bop background and decided to focus entirely on west coast swing 6 years ago. He has excellent technique and teaching skills.
Together they are constantly training in international workshops and take part in various competitions where they shine and have received countless trophies. They have been teaching regular classes in Paris and giving workshops in France since 2009.
Both passionate, they are willing to share their vision of west coast swing in a very friendly atmosphere.

For more details on Lena & Olivier: Temple du Swing.

Lena and Olivier dancing:

We have the pleasure of having them teach us two workshops:
  • 10.30 to 12.00: Get out of the beaten paths!
  • 13.00 to 14.30: Pattern and musical interpretation

The afternoon of social dancing will begin just after the end of the 2nd workshop and will finish around 6.30pm.
Workshops will be given in French, translation in English is possible if needed.

You have to be a member of Brussels WCS asbl to take part in Brussels WCS Workshops and practice (10€).

This workshops and practice will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
