Workshops and practice

June 21st 2015

Isaline, Sven and Sylvin invite you to the Maison de Quartier Malibran at 1pm for a free introduction to West Coast Swing.

This introduction is followed by an afternoon of training and social dancing until 6.30pm. You will then be able to see people who have been dancing WCS for several years share their passion on the dancefloor.

This event will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
