Workshops and practice

May 10th 2015 – Melena and Sven

Melena comes from the US and started dancing swing when she was 16, then trained in ballroom and latin dances. She competes in All Star division and is first and foremost a social dancer. She just moved to Amsterdam in the Netherlands and is developing a WCS community there. She is also working with Zouk teachers to develop Swouk, an interesting mix of Swing and Zouk.
She will be assisted by Sven, who is teaching regular classes with Brussels WCS and recently competing in the Advanced division.

We have the pleasure of having them teach us two workshops:
  • 10.30 to 12.00: Musicality & Partnering
  • 13.00 to 14.30: Variations and syncopations for basic patterns

The afternoon of social dancing will begin just after the end of the 2nd workshop and will finish around 6.30pm.
Workshops will be given in English with translation in French if needed.

You have to be a member of Brussels WCS asbl to take part in Brussels WCS Workshops and practice (10€).

This workshops and practice will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
