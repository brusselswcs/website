Workshops and practice

November 16th 2014 – Virginie and Sylvain

Virginie and Sylvain met on the parisian dancefloor when they started wcs in 2007. They had the same reaction: they immediately fell in love with this dance that brings feelings, offers creativity and play between partners. They went to various workshops and events with the best teachers and never left the dancefloor anymore.
Sylvain then started competing and now is in the Advanced division, while Virginie, well-known for not being the competing type, won the social dancer award on one of the French Open editions. They are pleased to be able to share their common passion and teach together in Paris.

We have the pleasure of having them teach us two workshops:
  • 10.00 to 11.30 - How to put contrast in your dance
  • 12.30 to 14.00 - Redirection

The afternoon of social dancing will begin just after the end of the 3rd workshop and will finish around 6.30pm.
Workshops will be given in French, translation in English is possible if needed.

You have to be a member of Brussels WCS asbl to take part in Brussels WCS Workshops and practice (10€).

This workshops and practice will take place at the Maison de Quartier Malibran.
